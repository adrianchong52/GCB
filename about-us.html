About us

At Global Coffee Barista,
Our Vision is to Empower Coffee Enthusiasts and Professionals Alike

We're on a mission to inspire and empower individuals, whether you're a coffee consumer, a coffee professional, an entrepreneur, or a cafe owner, to excel in the world of coffee.

Our educational offerings are meticulously crafted to help you expand your knowledge and elevate your business. Designed around the Specialty Coffee Association (SCA) Coffee Skills Program, our courses adhere to a certified curriculum, continuously refined by leading experts in the coffee industry.

What sets us apart is our team of Authorized SCA Trainers, seasoned coffee professionals, and experienced judges. Not only do they convey the curriculum's objectives with utmost professionalism, but they also bring their firsthand experience and passion for coffee to every session. Join us on a journey of coffee excellence.

Discover Diverse Coffee Classes 

Whether you're new to coffee or an experienced enthusiast, we offer programs for everyone, from baristas honing their skills to café owners looking to expand their expertise.

Meet the Team

Hugo Chang

Hugo is a globally certified Authorised Specialty Coffee Association Trainer (AST) and Certifier, holding prestigious world certifications from the Specialty Coffee Association (SCA) and Barista Guild of America. He's also a Licensed Q-Arabica Grader since 2015.

In addition to his extensive coffee expertise, Hugo's impact on the industry is evident through his contributions to the founding of BGA and the organization of multiple significant coffee events in Malaysia. He's garnered media attention on platforms like BFM, Capital FM, Bernama TV, Star Newspaper, Nourish Magazine, Flavours Magazine, and TravelWireAsia. Beyond coffee, Daniel excels as a seasoned Speaking Professional, Trainer, HR Consultant, and serves as the Managing Director of Direct Results Asia Pacific Consultancy Sdn Bhd.

Credentials:
- Authorised Specialty Coffee Association Trainer (AST)
- Authorised Specialty Coffee Certifier
- Licensed Q-Arabica Grader

Jonathan Ong

Jonathan, a seasoned coffee professional, achieved 2nd runner-up at Malaysia's First Battle of Barista. His journey in the coffee world has seen him compete, organize, and judge various competitions.

He's completed SCA's Barista Skills module up to the Professional Level and earned the title of Authorised SCA Trainer (AST). As a judge, Jonathan has lent his expertise to competitions across Southeast Asia, including the Cambodia Barista Championship in 2016, the Vietnam Barista Championship, and the China Latte Art Championship in 2017. His extensive judging experience includes roles as a Visual Judge, Sensory Judge, Technical Judge, and Head Judge for numerous national competitions.

Credentials:
- 2nd runner up at the First Battle of Barista in Malaysia
- Authorised SCA Trainer (AST)
- Judge at Cambodia Barista Championship 2016
- Judge at Vietnam Barista Championship
- Judge at China Latte Art Championship 2017
