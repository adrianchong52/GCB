For Beginners

At Global Coffee Barista, we provide a diverse range of courses designed to cater to your interests. Whether you want to unravel the intricate flavors of coffee or master the art of creating exquisite latte designs, our courses are tailored to propel your coffee journey to the next level.

Additionally, our classes are available in multiple languages, including Bahasa Malaysia, Mandarin, and English, ensuring accessibility for all.

Tailoring to
Your Coffee Passions.

Basic Latte Art

Suited For | Consumers
Duration | 4 hours

Every barista wants to be a great latte artist, but not many know where to start. Learn the essentials of frothing, pouring and etching in this session and be on your way to impressing yourself with your own Instagram-worthy latte art shots.

Cupping Protocol

Suited For | Consumers
Duration | 2 hours

Cupping coffee is probably one of the many essential skill anyone whether a coffee buyer, barista, roaster or even a consumer can develop within the coffee industry. You will learn how the judges determine what quality grade a varietal of coffee deserves. Experience the judging process with this course and heighten your knowledge on what differentiates good coffee, from bad coffee.

Espresso Sensory

Suited For | Consumers
Duration | 2 hours

Heighten your tastebuds with this essential course that captures the heart of the café - the espresso. It is the base of the latte, cappuccino, flat white and mocha, and may be our key source of caffeine in the mornings, but also the key to a great cup of coffee. Use all your senses to identify the many different flavors of coffee in this important course!

Junior Barista Workshop

Suited For | Teenagers (13-17 years old)
Duration | 6 hours

Learning shouldn’t be limited to the four walls of a classroom. Equip yourself with some basic barista skills and knowledge about coffee! Perhaps you might have a secret love for becoming a barista and serving coffee to others. You won’t know till you give it a try!

Brewing Method

Suited For | Consumers
Duration | 2 hours

Interested in manual brewing? This course teaches you the many different ways to brew at home! Understand the brewing concepts and parameters that will affect your cup of coffee and learn about the the brewing methods that brings out the most flavour and aroma of every bean.

Introduction to Coffee

Suited For | Consumers
Duration | 2 hours

We will take you through how coffee is cultivated, harvested and processed. We also help you to identify different roasting profiles as well as best practices in buying, storing and grinding coffee. You will also learn the fine art of brewing and how to evaluate a great cup of coffee and other brewing methods that will bring out the most flavor and aroma.

CSP Professional Barista Training

Suited For | Consumers
Duration | 1 day physical class / 2 Weeks online course

This SCA-approved module equips you with essential skills to excel as a barista, savvy coffee consumer, or potential cafe owner. It combines theory and practical elements, covering coffee history, latte art, grinder operation, safety measures, and customer service for a comprehensive foundation.
