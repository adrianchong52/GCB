Coffee Catering Service for Your Events

Elevate Your Event with Exceptional Coffee Service

A successful event deserves exceptional coffee to keep your guests refreshed and delighted. We offer comprehensive coffee catering services, providing top-notch equipment and skilled baristas to brew the finest coffee for your attendees.

When you choose us for your event, you'll enjoy:

A minimum of 100 cups of delicious coffee
One or more espresso machines (tailored to your event's size)
Experienced baristas to cater to your coffee needs
Complimentary coffee appreciation demonstrations (Sensory, Latte Art, etc.)

At Global Coffee Barista, our mission is to assemble a passionate team of coffee professionals who share our love for coffee and people. Beyond our initial coffee catering services at company events, we expanded to offer educational workshops for corporate staff. These workshops not only foster unique team-building experiences but also empower individuals with valuable new skills.
