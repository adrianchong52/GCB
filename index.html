Becoming a Barista?

A barista is often likened to a coffee industry engineer, not solely for crafting coffee but also for demanding qualities like patience, consistency, and precision in recipe development. Today, being a barista offers diverse career paths beyond the coffee bar, including roles as trainers, coffee roasters, green bean traders, and more.
